Site Purpose Statement

The purpose of this website is to promote and support a restaurant consulting business known as Top Tier Dining Group. The site provides information about consulting services offered to both new and existing restaurant owners. It also works as a lead generation tool by allowing users to request consultations through the contact page.

Accessibility Statement

Top Tier Dining Group is committed to providing an accessible website for all visitors. The site uses strong color contrast, readable fonts, clear headings, descriptive image alternative text, keyboard-friendly navigation, and properly labeled form fields. The website is also designed to respond to mobile and desktop screen sizes.
